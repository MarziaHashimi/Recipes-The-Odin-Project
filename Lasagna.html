Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna is made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion, garlic, salt, and pepper for seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan; season with onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  3. Layer the lasagna noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat the layers and top with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
